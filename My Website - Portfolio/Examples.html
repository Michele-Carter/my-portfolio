Pevious work examples

Below are a few examples of the work I did during my previous two courses with Yoobee - Certificate in Creative Media and Diploma in Graphic Design Level 6.

In the captions below the pictures, I've highlighted the programs used to create the content. I also used Adobe Flash to create a short cartoon film, Adobe Premiere Pro to create a short clip as a group task and also created my first website.

Prior to creating any of the below pices, or any work I did, I always created a mood boards to help create an over all feel and for the Website (plus others projects such as creating a book) drew up story boards to determine the contents and workflow, before starting any work on the pc.

I hope to be able to improve, update and refresh my existing knowledge.

Audio shop Logo

This logo was created for an online audio retail shop, aimed to target a younger male audience between the ages of 16 - 24 years of age, interested in in deep bass and clear sound. Created using Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop.

Kids pamphlet

A kids healthy eating pamphlet aimed at young children between the ages of 8 - 12 years of age. I decided on using bright colours to attract attention and created the children figures using Adobe Illustrator.

Speaker

This speaker was created in order to be used in the design of a cd cover for a band/singer and was also used to create the other speaker logo for the online audio retail shop. Created using Adobe Illustrator.

Magazine

A magazine created to target niche audiences who are into alternative theories. While a majority of the contents was done using Adobe InDesing, some image editing was done using Adobe Photoshop.
